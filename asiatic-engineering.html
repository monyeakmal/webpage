Our Story

Built on trust,
precision, and experience.

Asiatic Engineering was founded in 1992 with a simple but enduring mission — to deliver the finest air conditioning solutions in Mumbai without ever compromising on quality.

Over three decades, we have grown from a small Vikhroli-based operation into one of Mumbai's most respected HVAC companies, trusted by residential clients, leading hotels, hospitals, schools, and corporates.

What sets us apart is our fully in-house team. We don't outsource design or execution — every project is planned, engineered, and installed by our own specialists.

Our Approach

Designed first.
Executed perfectly.

Every project begins in our design studio. Our consultants analyse your space, calculate thermal loads, and produce a precise layout before a single unit is ordered. This design-first approach ensures efficient, aesthetically considered installations that last.

01
VRF & VRV Systems

Variable Refrigerant Flow and Volume systems are the gold standard for large commercial properties. We design, supply, and commission VRF/VRV systems for hotels, malls, hospitals, and corporate campuses with full post-installation support.

CommercialHotelsMallsHospitals
02
Residential Solutions

From window units to multi-room split systems, we bring the right climate solution to your home. Our team assesses your space and recommends the most energy-efficient option — then handles the full installation.

Window ACSplit ACCassetteFloor Standing
03
Maintenance & AMC

Preventive servicing is the most cost-effective way to extend the life of your HVAC system. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts, scheduled servicing, gas top-up, and rapid-response emergency call-outs across Mumbai.

Design & Engineering
